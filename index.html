WHAT IS CARBON FOOTPRINT

Carbon Footprint

Carbon footprint refers to the total amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide, emitted by an individual, organization, or product during its lifecycle.

Nick Faraday - CC-0

Climate Change

Carbon footprint is a major contributor to climate change, which has devastating effects on the planet, including rising temperatures, increased frequency of natural disasters, and loss of biodiversity.

YummyBear0 - CC-BY-SA

Health Impact

The burning of fossil fuels and other activities that produce carbon emissions can have significant health impacts, such as respiratory problems and increased risk of heart disease and stroke.

Julie Pency - CC-0

Environmental Degradation

Carbon footprint contributes to environmental degradation through activities such as deforestation, soil erosion, and pollution of waterways, which can have long-lasting effects on ecosystems.

Jason Beck - CC-BY-NC-SA
